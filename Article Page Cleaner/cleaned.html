Although having a passive similar to Cassie Cage/Prime, his allows a wider range of team-ups with characters of a specific class rather than with specific characters themselves. His basic combos, although slow, has an amazing knockback both blocked and unblocked. As such, he can juggle effectively.

,

When teamed up with Sub-Zero/Cryomancer, Scorpion/Ninjutsu, Johnny Cage/A-List and Sonya Blade/Demolition, he can have a high attack and health boost of +70% or if the player double-stack the two Martial Artists or Spec Ops members, the highest will be +100% attack or health. Despite what one may think, Kenshi's passive does not double even if he is teamed with two Spec Ops or two Martial Artist characters.

,

Please note that battles are highly situational: this section only mentions cards that have mechanics that, for any reason, are particularly effective with/against, or particularly ineffective against Kenshi. Having a card that "counters" Kenshi does not mean it is always a good choice.
